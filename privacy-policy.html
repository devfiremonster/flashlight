Privacy policy for Flashlight app

What does this app do?

This app allows you to turn on and off the camera flashlight on your Android device via a toggle switch displayed on the main screen.

What devices are supported by this app?

This app supports Android devices from API level 19 and greater, which corresponds to Android platform version 4.4 and version code KitKat and greater. For a full list of API levels, please see here.

What data is collected by this app?

No data is collected by this app.

What features of the device are accessed by this app?

In order to turn on and off the camera flashlight on your device, this app requests permission to use these two camera hardware features:
  • android.hardware.camera - The app uses the device's back-facing camera.
  • android.hardware.camera.flash - The app uses the flash feature that the device's camera supports.
For more information about Android device features, click here.
